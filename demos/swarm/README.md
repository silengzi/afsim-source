# swarm demo

### swarm200.txt
  A bomber drops one cruise missile that is the "leader" and then drops an additional
  200 cruise missiles that are "clients".  The 200 client agm cruise missiles follow 
  the leader but somewhat randomly. The purpose of the demo is to illustrate how
  to make scripts within a guidance computer.

### swarm_tracks.txt
  A radar sensor is positioned by the bullseye and tracks 11 incoming agm missiles.
  As the agm missile positions are constantly changing, only a subset of the missiles
  are actually tracked because of their close proximity with each other. The purpose
  of the demo is to illustrate track correlation and fusion along with showing the
  difference between perception and truth that Mystic only depicts with
  track lines. In addition, WsfDraw is extensively used to show the track lines as well
  as the platform contributors to the perceived tracks with errors.
